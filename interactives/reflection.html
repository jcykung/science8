Law of Reflection Review

Science 8 Interactive Sandbox & Quiz

The Law of Reflection: The angle of incidence (i) equals the angle of reflection (r). Both angles are measured from the Normal Line (a line exactly 90° to the surface).
Interact: Drag the yellow laser emitter. Drag the protractor and snap it to the mirror! Use the green handle to rotate it. Draw a box in empty space to zoom, then drag to pan!
Challenge: Drag the orange node to set the reflected ray. Match the incident angle (i) from the normal line!
